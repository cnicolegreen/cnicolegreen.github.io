ABOUT THREE RIVER RETRIEVERS

Welcome to our site! Our goal is to bring as much joy to your home as our Golden Retrievers have brought to ours. Life with a Golden is the most rewarding adventure you will ever have. Golden Retrievers love to play, snuggle and they will be your new best friend. They enjoy the outdoors as much as they do indoors. Golden Retrievers are one of the most loyal dogs bred.

REASONS TO OWN A GOLDEN RETRIEVER

  • Golden Retrievers are Compassionate
  • Golden Retrievers are Gentle
  • They are SMART! According to the AKC GR rank in the top 5 of dogs to breed/own and have as your family pet.
  • Training a Golden Retriever is easy due to their obedient nature
  • Golden Retrievers are loyal
  • Golden Retrievers love the water and to SWIM
  • It is not their nature to be frequent BARKERS!
  • They are VERY popular with families and incredibly loving to kids
  • They are low maintenance
  • Golden Retrievers are comforting by nature.
